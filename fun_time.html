Homophones:

Aloud / Allowed

  • Aloud: Means to speak or say something in a voice that can be heard.
    Sentence: The teacher asked the student to read the poem aloud to the class.
  • Allowed: Means to give permission or let someone do something.
    Sentence: We are not allowed to use phones during school hours.


Stationary / Stationery

  • Stationary: Means not moving or staying in one place.
    Sentence: The car remained stationary at the red light.
  • Stationery: Refers to writing materials like pens, pencils, and paper.
    Sentence: I bought new stationery for my art project.
